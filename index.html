Welcome

I wear many hats across both my professional and personal life. I am currently a college student majoring in Computing, while also pursuing minors in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and Criminology, which reflect my interest in technology and public service. Outside of school, I balance several responsibilities, including work, military service, and being a father. These roles require time, discipline, and flexibility, which shape my daily life and priorities.

This website is a simple introduction to who I am and what matters most to me. It focuses on the three areas that take up most of my time and energy: work, education, and fatherhood. Each of these parts of my life has helped shape my goals, my priorities, and the person I am becoming.

Site Overview

The Work page explains the jobs and responsibilities I manage outside of school, along with how those experiences have helped me grow. The Education page focuses on my college path, my field of study, and the academic goals I am working toward. The Fatherhood page highlights one of the most important parts of my life and shows how being a father influences my choices and priorities.

The quiz at the bottom of the page is designed to test how well visitors paid attention while reading through the site. The questions can cover basic facts about me, the roles I balance, and the main topics covered on each page. It adds a simple interactive feature to the website while also tying together the information presented throughout the site.
